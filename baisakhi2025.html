Baisakhi 2025

A humble effort to recreate the memories of Noboborsho in the heart of Greater Boston


Organized by: Hashi Khushi of Greater Boston

Date: 10th May

Time: 3PM – 9PM

Venue: Keefe Regional Technical H.S. 750 Winter St., Framingham

Maitreyee Chakroborty

Maitreyee Chakraborty, founder of Tagore, Me, and You, brings Tagore’s poetry and music to audiences through performances and teaching. She will be performing Tagore’s soulful compositions at our Baisakhi celebration.

A Tribute to Manoj Mitra: Mahabidya

Join us for Mahabidya, a satirical comedy by the legendary Manoj Mitra. With his blend of humor and social insight, Manoj Mitra had entertained audiences for decades. As a tribute to the celebrated playwright, we bring this classic to life on stage.

Dance Performances by Upasana

Upasana: A charming ensemble of committed Odissi dancers who thrive to preserve traditions while exploring innovations. They have been captivating audiences for the past one decade at festivals, fundraising concerts and community events. For the Baisakhi event, Upasana will be showcasing the many moods of Spring and the associated celebrations.

A tribute to Satyajit Ray by our kids

Enjoy a delightful musical journey as children sing the beloved songs from Satyajit Ray’s Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne films. These timeless tunes have enchanted generations, and our young performers bring them to life with joy and enthusiasm. A nostalgic treat for all!

Bengali Food Mela

Craving the taste of home? Enjoy a delicious spread of authentic Bengali food available for purchase from local vendors, including the popular Foodland.
