Chhobi Te Baisakh: Hashi Khushi's Baisakhi 2025 Kid's Art Exhibition

Let’s pass down the vibrant spirit of Bengali Naboborsho to the next generation! This kids' art exhibition is a heartfelt tribute to our roots, where young artists express the joy of family, friendship, and tradition through their creativity.

Theme: Baisakhi art exhibition

  • Naboborsho or Baisakhi themed painting or artwork that celebrates our culture, family and friendship
  • Painting or artwork size – up to 16” by 20”

Eligibility:

  1. 8 to 16 year old.
  2. Person or family must be registered and paid for event.
  3. One painting per participant. Can have multiple participants in the same family registration.
  4. First 20 entrants will be considered.
  5. Should confirm participation email or registration form by April 15th.
  6. Should be able to provide artwork by May 3rd.

Submission and return process:

  • We will coordinate collecting the artwork from participants via email.
  • You may take the artwork back with you at the end of the event.

What we will need besides artwork:

  • We plan to display the artwork to recognize and give credit to the creator, but the details provided are at the discretion of the creators and their families/guardians.
  • We are asking for:
    • Name
    • Town
    • School name
    • Grade

Disclaimer:

While we will take utmost care of the artwork, we are not responsible for any damage. In the least, we may need to pin them to the exhibition wall. Thank you for your understanding.
